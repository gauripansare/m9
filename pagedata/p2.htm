Background

You are meeting a group of friends at a new local café called "Coffee Rack". You arrive early to beat traffic but need to reply to some email and check some online transactions before your friends arrive. You plan to take advantage of the café’s wireless Internet for customers because your laptop has an embedded wireless card. The café has provided the network information on a poster on the wall. The network is called TCR. The network security key is CoffeeRules!

After booting up, click the To_Do icon on the desktop to review the list of tasks that you still have to complete today. Once you do that, you are ready to connect and begin.
